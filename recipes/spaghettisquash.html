Roast Spaghetti Squash

Roast your own spaghetti squash as a carb-friendly alternative to pasta

Ingredients
  • Spaghetti Squash
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Cut spaghetti squash lengthwise
  3. Rub inside of squash with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper
  4. Place cut side down on baking sheet lined with foil
  5. Bake for 35-55 minutes depending on preference (Shorter time is firmer "noodle")
