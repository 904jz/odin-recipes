Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Melty cheese on perfectly toasted bread

Ingredients
  • 2 slices of your choice of bread
  • 2-3 slices of your cheese of choice (American works best)
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Preheat a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Butter the "outside" of two pieces of bread
  3. Build your sandwich
  4. Place your sandwich in the pan
  5. Flip when first piece of bread is golden brown
  6. Remove from pan when bottom side is golden brown
  7. Slice diagonally and enjoy
