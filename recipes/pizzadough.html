Pizza Dough

Homemade pizza dough from scratch

Make your own pizza dough from scratch with this easy recipe

Ingredients
  • 3 cups of Flour (I use bread flour)
  • 1 packet of instant dried yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • pinch of sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • olive oil
Steps
  1. Pour water into stand mixer with yeast, olive oil and sugar
  2. While yeast blooms, sift flour and salt into a container
  3. Turn mixer on low and slowly add flour
  4. Mix for about 15 minutes until dough is one ball
  5. Transfer dough into bowl greased with olive oil
  6. Cover with plastic wrap or a damp cloth
  7. Allow to rest until doubled in size
  8. Divide into equal parts and refrigerate until ready to use
